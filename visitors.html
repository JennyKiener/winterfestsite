Nate Zobrist

He told us to be passionate about what we do. Because you can be a programmer and have a good income but when you don’t have fun and don’t like it, then it will lead to stress and depression. So when he interviews people, he asks them about their hobbies and what they do in their free time. And when you have little side projects where you work on in your free time. Another very big thing he told and showed us is how many coding languages there are. There are too many to learn all of them but when you know some of the basics then it isn’t that difficult to learn others anymore. At the end he also told us that programmers are always requested in our job life so we won’t have to worry about a good income and getting a job easily.

Hector Piña

“Becoming a programmer is becoming a lifetime student”, that is a sentence that he got from his mentor in his 11 week coding camp. He is very passionate in what he is doing and said that there is a super bowl moment in every solved problem. Hector is someone that knows, how much programming can make you feeling down. But he just set the rule that he won’t work at weekends and thus takes a lot of vacations. That doesn’t mean that he does no programming in his vacation. He told us that he once was vacuuming when the solution to a problem of his coding came to his mind and he just ran to his laptop and solved it. So he didn’t actually think about the problem but his brain solved it without his consciousness. Another thing he mentioned was to use google as often as we can because we don’t want to bug our teammate when there is a fast solution on google. Well, in the end he told us to take our time to play, so to take some time off the screen and do something we enjoy.

Mr. Olsen

Mr. Olsen, the previous german teacher of Maeser, came in and told us about his tips in programming. One of them was to draw out ideas out and AFTER that we should start programming our website. Then we should make it as non confusing as possible so that we have a good overview over our own code (sometimes it can get super confusing and that leads to a mistake in the code).


Mr. Ensign

So when Mr. Ensign came in, he told and explained to us how to make our website with a very good design. The main essence is to only use 2-3 colors -”keep it as simple as possible”. And when you break that rule, do it with a good reason.


Mr. Heaton

Ryan Heaton works at the Family Search and is there to teach computers how to read old/ancient documents. That means he has to teach it how to read all those old fonts which aren’t easy to learn. But still, nothing is impossible as long as we reach for our dreams. For real job stability you need to become the best programmer that is wanted by many companies so that if you lose your job, you still would get one within one week. Another very important point in programming is your creativity and the “thinking outside of the box.” An advantage of the digital documents of 100 years ago is that they don’t take in as much space as the normal paper ones. Furthermore there is access from all over the world. There is no limit to what Technology can do, there is a limit to our imagination. Another job except programming that is very wanted is Cypersecurity specialists. There are a lot of dangers in the world but the one of the internet is even bigger and more dangerous.
